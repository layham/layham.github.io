Hadleigh Community News

Hadleigh Community News is a lively and informative free publication distributed to all homes in the Parish. It advertises and records most things that happen in and around Hadleigh. There are a couple of pages dedicated to Layham (Letter from Layham). If you have any service to offer, then an advertisement in the HCN reaches over 7,000 local homes. Each month this publication can be found at www.hadleighcommunitynews.co.uk, where you can find an edited version of Letter from Layham.
